Achievements and Activities


TCS ION Intelligem winners!
Our school has had three winners each year for two consecutive years now, in the TCS ION Intelligem 2019-2020 and 2020-2021. It was a proud moment for all of us, as each winner received a cash prize of Rs. 50,000.


Hosts the annual Teacher's Quiz
We have the proud pleasure of hosting the annual Teacher's Quiz, which is a friendly yet healthy competition between the teachers of various schools on many topics. It is in our foundation to not only celebrate our students but also our teachers!



Bask N Glory- Basketball Tournament
We ensure not only academic but as well as extra-cirrucalar and sports excellence among our students. Bask N Glory is a fine example to that, within various basketball teams of schools perform annually for the cup!




What's New?



  • New School App!

    We have now a new school app in addition to this website! We are currently using the CoolG App to database our student's academic progress. By logging in to CoolG with your credentials, you can now track you ward's learning plan, any due payments towards the school, your child's report cards, exam schedules , homework and more! Please also keep a regular check on the app, as we are going to post any important circular regarding the school and your ward in the NoticeBoard section of the app. Many schools have been successfully databasing on this app for years now and we expect the same!



  • Our Principal Changes..

    We now welcome Mrs. Anita Vinodkumar as our new principal. But, with a sad heart, we bid farewell to Mrs. Rama Ranganathan, our founding principal. Managing the school for over 10 years, we also greatly thank her for making our school what it is today. We ensure to the parents that the change in school faculty, wouldn't lead to any change in our principals, foundation and efficiency. We, once again thank the parents for their support and trust in us.
